# Adabiyot AI — Premium Uzbek Digital Literature App

A cinematic, curated literature platform that blends a digital library, audiobooks, short literary videos, and an author encyclopedia — built in Uzbek, with a dark luxurious aesthetic.

## Design direction

- **Mood**: Deep charcoal-black canvas with warm ivory text, signature crimson red (#ED1C24) accents, and muted bronze-sand (#A59D8B) highlights. Feels like a literary cinema — not a typical app.
- **Typography**: Elegant serif for book titles, poems, and reading; clean modern sans-serif for all UI.
- **Texture**: Subtle grain, soft gradients, glassmorphic overlays on reels, gentle shadows, generous spacing, large rounded corners.
- **Motion**: Smooth fades, parallax on hero banners, spring-based card presses, shimmer on featured items, haptic taps.
- **App icon**: Matte black tile with an embossed serif "A" in crimson red, framed by a thin bronze border — feels like a rare book spine.

## Features

- **Home tab**: Cinematic featured book hero with parallax, "Trend" row, horizontal viral reels preview, personalized recommendations, free reads section, featured authors carousel, and featured publishers strip.
- **Explore tab**: Elegant search bar, 8-category grid (Hikoya, Roman, She'r, Qo'llanma, Darslik, Ertak, Ssenariy, Qissa), filter chips (Bepul, Pullik, Audio mavjud, Trend, Yangi), and trending results.
- **Reels tab**: Full-screen vertical literary videos with autoplay, right-side like/comment/save/share, left-side author + publisher + follow button, a "Haqida" glass card with short description, and a "Batafsil" sheet that opens full info with Read / Buy / View Author actions.
- **Library tab**: Tabs for Sotib olingan (Purchased), Saqlangan (Saved), Reels, and Tarix (History) with beautiful book-spine-style lists.
- **Profile tab**: User avatar, reading stats, achievements/badges row, purchased content shelf, and settings.
- **Book page**: Large cinematic cover with blurred backdrop, title, clickable author, description, star rating, and Read Preview / Buy / Author buttons.
- **Reader**: Distraction-free dark reading view with font size control, bookmark toggle, chapter progress, and smooth scrolling.
- **Audio + karaoke player**: Premium audio player with animated waveform, speed control (1x / 1.5x / 2x), and word-by-word highlighted poem text that auto-scrolls in sync with a sample poem.
- **Author profile (encyclopedia)**: Portrait, curated biography, list of works, reads/followers stats, and Follow button — non-editable, encyclopedic feel.
- **Mock content**: Modern contemporary Uzbek authors and poets with realistic titles, covers, and excerpts.

- **Audio player** — karaoke-synced poem experience.
- **Author profile** — encyclopedia-style biography and works.

## Language

All UI copy in Uzbek (Bosh sahifa, Qidirish, Reels, Kutubxona, Profil, Haqida, Batafsil, O'qish, Sotib olish, Kuzatish, etc.).

## Out of scope for v1

Real uploads, real payments, real authentication, backend certificates/QR generation, and actual video/audio files — all represented with elegant mock data and sample media so the whole app is navigable and feels production-quality.
